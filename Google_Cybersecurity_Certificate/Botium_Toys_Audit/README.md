# Botium Toys Internal Security Audit

**Program:** Google Cybersecurity Certificate
**Completed:** May 2026

## Overview

This is an internal security audit conducted for Botium Toys, a fictional small U.S. toy company with a growing online presence. The audit was completed as a portfolio activity for the Google Cybersecurity Certificate program.

## What Was Audited

- Controls assessment across administrative, technical, and physical control categories
- Compliance evaluation against PCI DSS, GDPR, and SOC Type 1 and Type 2 frameworks
- Risk assessment review based on the IT manager's scope and goals report

## Key Findings

- Least privilege and separation of duties not implemented
- No encryption for credit card data
- No disaster recovery plan or data backups
- No intrusion detection system installed
- Weak password policy with no centralized enforcement
- Physical controls including locks, CCTV, and fire systems are in place
- 72-hour EU breach notification plan exists

## Skills Demonstrated

- Internal security auditing
- Risk assessment analysis
- Controls assessment
- Compliance evaluation (PCI DSS, GDPR, SOC)
- Written recommendations to stakeholders
